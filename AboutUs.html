About me


I'm Isuri Gunatilake, a certified tour guide in Japan.

I have more than 6 years of expereince in guiding tourists. Tourist Guiding is not just my job but also my passion. I love travelling around the world and showing people the beauties of the world. After sometime, the beauty and the culture of Japan drew me in, and I finally settled here as a proffessional tourist guide in Japan.

My purpose is to show visitors like you the beauty and culture of Japan. Having worked with different clients from all over the world, I am extremely fluent in several languages like English, Japaneses, French and German.

Having pursued a Higher Diploma & a Degree in Tourism, I'm well versed about the cultural aspects of Japan. In 2015.2016 and 2018 I was awarded the "Best tourist Guide" from the International Tourist Association of Japan.
