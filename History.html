History of the Church

• The church was built by a rich person in Moratuwa with the name Kariyakarawana Mahapatabandige Don Bonifacio Ferdinando.

• He had issues with a court case, and he made a vow to St. Francis Xavier that if he won the court case, he would built a church for the saint

• He won the court case and decided to build a church.

• Firstly the land to build the church was decided to be taken from the Rathmalana area, but since that land was not large enough, the land on which the current church is built had been bought by Mr. Ferdinando.

• When the head stone was laid the Supreme Pontiff of the Catholic Church was Pope Pius IX, the Archbishop of the archdiocese of Colombo was Most Rev. Dr. Hillarian Sillani O.S.B.

• The head stone was laid by Rev. Fr. Joseph Vanderistratan in 3rd of December 1869.

• Then the work was finished and the church was blessed open in 2nd December 1870 and the first Feast Mass was celebrated on Saturday the 3rd December 1870.

• The people who worked hard to build this church.

• U.B. Cadjaton, Bolonne, U. Peter Perera, Senven Perera, U. Jeronis Perera, M.M. Perera, G.M. Silva, P. Romiel Silva, Muhuppu M. Maria Perera, M. Stephen Fernando, K.G. Fernando, M. Jochim Peiris, M. Gregory Peiris, M. Anthony Perera, W.O.A. de Mel, W. Jeromius Fernando. R.S. Fernando, W. Anamaria de Mel.

• Earlier the Masses were celebrated only on the Feast Days.

• Choir on Novena Days – Cadjaton Peiris and his team.

• Choir on Feast Day – Lawrence Perera and his team.

• 25th Jubilee on 1895; 50th Jubilee 1920.

• From 1933, Rev. Fr. Wilfred Piguradu started celebrating Mass on every second and fourth Sunday of the month.

• The first choir was formed in 1934 by Rev. Fr. Marjes.

• Rev. Fr. Burgeon started celebrating Mass on every Sunday

• A carnival was held from 9th to 14th April 1945 to raise funds for the 75th Jubilee

• 75th Jubilee was celebrated by Rev. Fr. A. Yomni

• Rev. Fr. Burgeon who was reappointed as Parish Priest started celebrating Mass even on the Days of Obligation

• Then he moved on to continue the right most part of the church-school

• The Legion of Mary was formed by Rev. Fr. Peter Shinas as a Sinhalese speaking presidium

• Rev. Fr. John Gomes started celebrating Mass on every Wednesday and first Friday of the month

• A two day fair was organized by the church for the 100th Jubilee

• 100th Jubilee was celebrated in 1970 by Rev. Fr. Basil A. Weerathunga OMI, the Parish Priest was Rev. Fr. Anselem

• In 1979, the Rawatawatta Parish was separated from the Moratuwa Parish, and both Angulana and Lunawa churches were taken under the Parish of Rawatawatta

• In 1985, the first church was abolished and head stone was laid along with a wall around

• A Youth Society was formed in 6th September 1987

• The purpose of forming the Youth Society was to obtain the financial aid to rebuild the church. They did projects such as lottery, selling bricks and a carol to raise funds.

• The Youth Society started praying the Holy Hours in zonal levels

• St. Vincent de Paul’s society was started 16th November 1987

• The concrete pillars for the new church were constructed

• Archbishop Most Rev. Dr. Nicholus Marcus Fernando suggested that the church should have a Pastoral Council, and Rev. Fr. Nimal Ponweera was able to establish it within two weeks

• In 1989, the roof for the new church was constructed

• The first Mass in the new church was celebrated by Very Rev. Fr. Francis, the Episcopal Vicar of Kaluthara

• In 1990, construction of the walls of the church were started

• In 1990, the Festive Mass was celebrated by Rev. Fr. Morris Leslie Perera

• In 1991, Rev. Fr. Edward Ravelpulle completed the walls, doors and windows were completed and temporary electricity connection was taken

• The Legion of Mary was started in 1991

• The Sunday school building was renovated in 1992

• In 1992, the Liturgy Committee and Building Committee was formed

• In 1993, the floor was cemented

• In 1994, the sanctuary was tiled

• In 1995, the Society of the Sacred Heart was formed

• In 1995, the 125th Jubilee were celebrated. The Vespers was celebrated by Rev. Fr. Elmo Dias, and the Jubilee Mass was celebrated by the Archbishop Most. Rev. Dr. Nicholus Marcus Fernando

• In 2006, a new Sunday School building was constructed along with a mission house

• In 9th September 2018, under Fr. Edrich Rodrigue head stone was laid to construct a new façade

• The Jubilee Mass was celebrated in 5th December 2021 by His Eminence Most Rev. Dr. Malcolm Cardinal Ranjith.
